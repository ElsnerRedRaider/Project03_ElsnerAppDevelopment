A studio built around one idea

Software should treat people's most important records with the same seriousness they do. That's the guiding principle on which Elsner App Development Services is built.

I started this studio to build small, well-documented software products that enable clients to trace every feature back to a real problem and a real decision, not simply a list of features. HomeProof, our first product, is where that approach is brought to fruition.

Featured Case File

HomeProof

Status: Prototype

HomeProof is an AI-powered home inventory app. It quickly identifies, and accurately documents, what clients own, photographing and identifying belongings so there's a clear and trustworthy record when it matters most.

The first audience is insurance carriers, where a fast and accurate inventory makes claims smoother for all parties involved. HomeProof is also valuable in legal and divorce proceedings, where a shared, credible record of property can make a difficult process a little more fair and a lot less hassle.
